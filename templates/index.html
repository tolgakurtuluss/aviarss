AviRSS Service

Your comprehensive web-service for generating and filtering RSS feeds based on IATA codes of airports worldwide.

About AviRSS

AviRSS is designed to provide users with the latest news and updates from airports around the world. Whether you're a frequent traveler, an aviation enthusiast, or just curious about airport activities, AviRSS offers a user-friendly platform to access real-time information.

How to Use

To generate an RSS feed, follow these steps:

  1. Use the following endpoint: https://aviarss.onrender.com/rss/{iata_code}
  2. Replace {iata_code} with the desired IATA code.
  3. Example:

    To get the RSS feed for Istanbul Airport, use:

    https://aviarss.onrender.com/rss/IST
