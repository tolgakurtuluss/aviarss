Welcome to the RSS Service

Your web-service for generating and filtering RSS feeds based on IATA codes of airports.

How to Use

To generate an RSS feed, follow these steps:

  1. Use the following endpoint:

    https://aviarss.onrender.com/rss/{iata_code}

  2. Replace {iata_code} with the desired IATA code.

  3. Example:

    To get the RSS feed for Istanbul Airport, use:

    https://aviarss.onrender.com/rss/IST
